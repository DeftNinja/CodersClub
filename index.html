Vision and Goals

Encouraging Young Enthusiastic Programmers

For India to be 100% Digital we need to educate each and every person about technology trends. Young generations of today will be leading the country in future. Hence they should know importance of technology and it's perfect use or it might get used for wrong purpose intentionally or unintentionally.

A Community Built By Coders, For Fellow Coders

An initiative taken by few Engineering students of FCRIT to develop coding habits in current and future generations. This website can help FCRIT students and other college students to interact with each other as well as develop as a programmer.
